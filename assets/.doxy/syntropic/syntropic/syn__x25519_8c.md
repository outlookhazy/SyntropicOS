## Detailed Description


Based on the TweetNaCl approach: 16 × int64\_t limbs (16 bits each). Simpler and more auditable than 10-limb donna at the cost of some speed. The entire scalar multiplication takes ~10M multiplies.


On Cortex-M4 @ 64 MHz this is ~2-4 seconds — acceptable since handshakes only happen every ~2 minutes.
